Recipe for Indomie

Description

This is a recipe to make a simple Indomie Dish

Ingredients

  • 2 Pack of Indomie Instant Noodles
  • 2 Teaspoon of Groundnut Oil
  • A Small Pot almost half-filled with Water
  • A Sprinkle of Powdered Pepper

Steps

  1. Put the pot of water on the gas cooker for 10min or when it's about to boil
  2. Pour the Teaspoons of Groundnut Oil in the pot
  3. Put the Indomie Noodles inside the pot and add the seasoning included in the wrapper
  4. Add the Pepper
  5. Wait 8-10min until the Noodles gets soft and seperates. Turn it around with a fork or whatever
  6. Stir and wait a few minutes. Switch of the gas cooker
  7. Serve in a plate or whatever and cry because it probably tastes like shit.
