Recipe for Baked Orange Salmon with Fennel

Description

This is a recipe to make a Baked Orange Salmon with Fennel Dish

Ingredients

  • 1 small fennel bulb, trimmed and diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 (8 ounce) salmon fillets
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 pinch Old Bay Seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (150 degrees C).
  2. In a medium skillet over medium heat, cook fennel in olive oil until translucent, about 20min.
  3. Place the fillets skin side down in a glass baking dish. Pout the orange juice over the fillets. Sprinkle fennel over in an even layer, and seaon with OLD BAY. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake for 20 to 25min, or until fish flakes easily with a fork.
